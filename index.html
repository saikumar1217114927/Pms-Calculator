Notes:

1In the illustration, Management fee is assumed to be charged annually. However, the Portfolio Manager charges fee Monthly/Quarterly as defined in the Fee Schedule which is part of PMS agreement
2Management Fees and Other Expenses are to be calculated on the average daily portfolio value payable on a monthly/quarterly basis at the end of each calendar month/quarter, as may be defined in Fee schedule.
3Returns are assumed to be generated linearly through the year.
4Other Expenses includes Account Opening charges, stamp duty /Audit Fee/ Bank charges / Fund Accounting charges / Custody Fee / demat charges or other miscellaneous expense, as may be defined in Fee schedule.
5Brokerage and transaction cost for the illustration purpose is charged on the Average AUM. However, Brokerage and Transaction cost are charged on basis the actuals trades.
6All Fees and charges are subject to GST.
7For this illustration, High Water Mark for the 1st Year is the Capital invested and from second year onwards if performance fee is charged, it's the year end closing value after all charges and fees, else it remains the same, if applicable
8For this illustration, Hurdle rate is calculated on Higher of (HWM or previous year closing capital). However, in actual Hurdle Rate of return is defined in the Fee Schedule and may differ from this illustration, if applicable
9Hurdle rate is prorated in case the performance fee period is less than 1 year OR if there are inflow/outflows from the portfolio during the computation period, if applicable
10If applicable, the Performance linked fee shall be charged on the basis of high water mark principle over the life of the investment. Value of the portfolio shall be taken to be the value on the date when the performance fee was charged. The performance linked fee shall be charged only on increase in portfolio value in excess of previously achieved high water mark and performance fees will be charges on annual basis or at the time of redemption, as applicable.
11Average daily portfolio value means the value of the portfolio of each client determined in accordance with the relevant provisions of the agreement executed with the client and includes both realized and unrealized gains/ losses.
12The "Profit share of the PMS" is equal to "Profit Share To be taken by PMS" with an assumption that the client is on direct mode.
13In addition to the above expense/fee, exit load shall be charged on the Post Performance fee AUM, if applicable as per the fee schedule.
